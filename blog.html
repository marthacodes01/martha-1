THE BIG FIVE

The big five animals are the standouts of any Africa Safari, the wildlife that for many define what a journey to the savannah is all about. The big five animals are originally the animals species that are difficult to hunt like:
The lion
The leopard
The rhino
The elephant
The African buffalo

Birds of Prey

Raptors can be identified by their hook-shaped beaks and sharp curved claws called talons. They are often called birds of prey because they pursue or prey on other smaller animals as their food source.
The Hawk
The owl
The Falcon
The vulture
